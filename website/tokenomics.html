A Streamlined Token Ecosystem

Based on the latest best practices for digital assets in mid-2025, we've revised our strategy to a robust, legally-aware three-part model. This new ecosystem is designed for simplicity, consolidated utility, and clear separation between fungible utility and non-fungible assets to maximize user engagement and build sustainable long-term value.

The Revised Token Ecosystem

The Asset Layers: Experience & Resilience NFTs

These remain as distinct, non-fungible assets, as their function is fundamentally different from the fungible $ECOSTAY token.

  • Experience NFTs: Access keys for all resort programming (e.g., yoga retreats, workshops). Post-event, they can become digital collectibles (POAPs) that may grant future perks.
  • Resilience NFTs: High-value, asset-backed tokens representing a deed or a legal right-to-access contract for the apocalypse storehouses. They are a distinct digital asset class.

Core Mechanic: Staking $ECOSTAY for Real Utility

The staking mechanism is based on tangible rewards for network participation, not passive income. Lock your $ECOSTAY tokens to earn "Staking Power" and unlock tiered benefits.

Tier Staking Requirement Key Benefits
Bronze 50 $ECOSTAY 10% discount on food & beverage, early-bird access to event announcements.
Silver 250 $ECOSTAY All Bronze benefits + priority booking for retreats, complimentary room upgrade (when available).
Gold 1,000 $ECOSTAY All Silver benefits + 1 free Event NFT airdrop per year, significant DAO voting weight.
Platinum 5,000 $ECOSTAY All Gold benefits + guaranteed access to purchase Resilience NFTs, direct line to community managers.

Time-Lock Multiplier

To reward commitment, users who lock their tokens for longer periods (e.g., 6, 12, or 24 months) will receive a multiplier on their Staking Power, granting them higher-tier benefits with fewer tokens.

Protocol Yield

A percentage of resort revenues (e.g., 5% of non-$ECOSTAY payments) will be programmatically converted to $ECOSTAY on the open market and distributed pro-rata to stakers as a reward for securing the network's economic flywheel.

Legal & Regulatory Strategy

This model is explicitly designed to align with the SEC's guidance from mid-2025.

  • Utility First: All marketing will focus on the token's utility for booking, access, and discounts. Language implying "investment" or "expectation of profit" will be strictly avoided.
  • No Fixed Returns: Staking yields are not guaranteed and are derived from protocol activity, consistent with SEC clarifications.
  • Clear Disclosures: All terms will be transparent. Users will be made aware they are participating in a network with a utility token, not purchasing a financial instrument.
