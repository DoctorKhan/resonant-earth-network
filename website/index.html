Palawan Flagship Ecovillage

Our flagship 20-bed ecovillage in Palawan, Philippines will serve as the blueprint for regenerative living. Featuring cutting-edge sustainability systems and holistic wellness facilities.

üåø Zone 1

Coastal Wellness & Gathering Zone

The vibrant heart of the ecovillage overlooking Palawan's turquoise coast. Sculptural bamboo architecture rises organically from lush tropical gardens, featuring the Surf-to-Soil Wellness Center with wave-inspired lines and an airy Meditation & Mindfulness Pavilion crowned by a lotus dome skylight.

üåø Zone 2

Gathering, Ceremony & Culinary Spaces

The ecovillage's cultural and culinary heart features a striking geodesic dome serving as the central Festival & Concert Center for music, art, and celebration. An open-air Meditation Pavilion with sculptural bamboo roof offers space for mindfulness and ceremony, while the Group Dining Area with long wooden tables under shaded canopies brings the community together for shared meals.

üåø Zone 3

Regenerative Farming & Infrastructure Zone

The ecovillage's regenerative core set in a lush tropical landscape. A large geodesic greenhouse dome anchors a network of permaculture gardens for year-round organic cultivation. Green-roofed bamboo buildings support farm operations, while the Zero Microplastic Water Center ensures clean water stewardship. Solar panels and wind turbines generate renewable energy, all connected by winding paths to the turquoise coastline.

üåø Zone 4

Business & Technology Hub

The ecovillage's creative and technological nucleus features a spacious two-story coworking center with bamboo and glass architecture, solar-integrated curved roofs, and open terraces. Outdoor meeting spaces, private bamboo pods, and a circular Web3 Governance Zone with digital dashboards empower collaboration, innovation, and decentralized decision-making in a nature-integrated setting.
